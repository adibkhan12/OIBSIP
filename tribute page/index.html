Michael Jackson

1958 - 2009

"The greatest education in the world is watching the masters at work."

Biography

Michael Jackson, also known as the 'King of Pop', was one of the most influential and successful musicians in history. His career spanned over four decades, producing legendary hits such as Billie Jean, Thriller, and Beat It.

With his unique voice, iconic dance moves, and groundbreaking music videos, he redefined the music industry. He won numerous awards, including 13 Grammy Awards and 26 American Music Awards.

Beyond music, Jackson was a philanthropist, donating millions to charities and supporting humanitarian causes worldwide.
